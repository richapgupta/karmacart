About Us

We are Karmacart Social Foundation an NGO from Mumbai, India, working towards providing free Medical & Healthcare services to the underprivileged. At the time of the COVID-19 first wave, we felt helpless. Everyone wanted to contribute something, but we couldn’t do it.

And now there is a time we can do a lot of things for this we just need to take the action which will create miraculous results for the needy people. Let’s come together and say “we can and we will do it” We firmly believe that every individual deserves the best Medical & Health care services, which is why we are fiercely committed to providing free Medical & Health care services.

With this bold & powerful ambition, we shall do whatever it takes to save the precious lives of one of the most intelligent creatures created by God.

All Donations to karmacart social foundation are 50% Tax exempt under section 80G of IT Act, 1961.

Power of Three Medical Free

We are coming with a project called Power Of Three Medical Free. For this, we need your support.

Our mission is to construct a 10-bed hospital & providing free medical treatment to the underprivileged.

For this, we are asking a donation of 1111/- per person.

And the support we need from you is that you enrolling 3 people to donate Rs 1111/- only and they getting 3 more people to donate Rs 1111/- so on for this noble cause.

We are asking you to enroll 3 people because we want you to be a part of this Noble Cause. By which you can know what power you have and the transformation you can bring in someone’s life.

The concept of getting 3 people with you is because together we can achieve whatever we want and the power lies within you to make this dream come true. So let's come together in making this dream a reality.

Your faith and unwavering support inspire us to work hard in providing free medical treatment to a large number of people. Our absolute transparency allows you to donate, contribute to society, and Volunteer in our programs. Thus, you get the first-hand experience of the difference you are making in the lives of others.

Volunteers

Terms & Conditions
Privacy Policy -
We are serious about protecting our members by addressing potential privacy concerns. Our privacy guidlines apply to all the members.
Personal information like your names, address, e-mail id, telephone number etc collected during enquiry, signup for newsletter or volunteer is not distributed, shared, rented or sold to companies or organizations that are not part of the Karmacart Social Foundation.
If you feel you are being contacted too often, please let us know and we shall limit to fewer updates or remove you from the mailing list, if you prefer so.
You may request a copy of the personal information you have shared with us, please drop an email contact@karmacartfoundation.org with a "Subject-Request for Personal Information ". We will send the personal details at the earliest.
If any personal information changes, you are requested to share the updated information to us at the earliest.
We ensure that your personal information is held securely, in accordance with the Data Protection Act.
Tax exemption details -
All Donations to Karmacart Social Foundation are eligible for 50% Tax Exempt under section 80G of IT Act, 1961.
Refund Policy -
Thank you for visiting our website and making donation. Our policy on cancellation & refund of the received donation for the cause is as under:-
a) Karmacart Social Foundation takes the utmost care to process donations as per the instructions given by our donors, online and offline. However, in case of an unlikely event of an erroneous donation or if the donor would like to cancel his donation, Karmacart Social Foundation will respond to the donor within 7 working days of receiving a valid request for refund from the donor.
The timely refund of the donation will depend upon the type of credit card/banking instrument used during the transaction.
The donor will have to send Karmacart Social Foundation a written request for a refund within 2 days of making the donation to CRY’s official address or email contact@karmacartfoundation.org along with-
i. Proof of the deduction of the donation amount.
ii. In cases where the donation receipt has already been issued to the donor, the donor will have to return the original receipt to us at our office addresses.
iii. If the tax exemption certificate is already issued, then we are sorry but we will not be able to refund the donation.
b) All the donations are through online payment gateway only.
c) If any in-kind support received by the donor from any where it shall reach to the poorest of the poorer communities.
d) The donation will be used for the providing free Health Care services to under privileged.
e) Corporate & CSR donations done Off-line are requested to submit Organisation PAN details through email.
