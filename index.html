About Us

We are Karmacart Social Foundation an NGO from Mumbai, India, working towards providing free Medical & Healthcare services to the underprivileged. At the time of the COVID-19 first wave, we felt helpless. Everyone wanted to contribute something, but we couldn’t do it.

And now there is a time we can do a lot of things for this we just need to take the action which will create miraculous results for the needy people. Let’s come together and say “we can and we will do it” We firmly believe that every individual deserves the best Medical & Health care services, which is why we are fiercely committed to providing free Medical & Health care services.

With this bold & powerful ambition, we shall do whatever it takes to save the precious lives of one of the most intelligent creatures created by God.

Power of 3

Our donors are the true drivers of change. They understand the impact we are determined to create. We bring in the power of 3 wherein each donor will bring in 3 more donors by the way of commitment, enrolment, and freedom of expression. You enrolling 3 people to donate Rs 1111/- for the project Power of three medical free and they getting more 3 people to donate Rs 1111/- so on for this NOBLE CAUSE. We are asking you to enroll 3 people because we want you to be part of this Noble cause.

By which you can know what power you have and the transformation you can bring in someone's life. Their faith and unwavering support inspire us to work hard in providing free Medical & Health care. Our absolute transparency allows them to donate, contribute to society and Volunteer our programs. Thus, they get the first-hand experience of the difference they are making in the lives.

Volunteers

Volunteers in other word Hero. Working with dedication and in integrity towards achieving the dream. Volunteers are our strength and pillars of the NGO . They are the face of the organisation. And they have the power to create the impossible. You are our true partners, ambassadors and inspiration .

You work in partnership with the organisation for a cause that will transform the society at large without any appreciation or rewards. In other words you are the commandos of the organisation who is constantly working for the other people.

Partners we salute you for who are and who you are not.
